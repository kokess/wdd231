Lagos Food Finder – Site Plan

Site Name

Lagos Food Finder

This name was chosen because it clearly represents a platform that helps users discover food and restaurants in Lagos quickly and easily.

Site Purpose

The Lagos Food Finder website will provide information about local foods, restaurants, and vendors in Lagos. It will allow users to explore popular dishes, find restaurant locations, and view prices using dynamic data from JSON and APIs.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find affordable local food near me in Lagos?
  • What are the most popular Nigerian dishes and where can I try them?

Color Schema

  • #004080 (Dark Blue): Used for headers, navigation, and footer.
  • #ffcc00 (Gold): Used for buttons and highlights.
  • #f4f4f4 (Light Gray): Used for page background.
  • #ffffff (White): Used for cards and content sections.

Typography

The website will use Roboto from Google Fonts.

  • Headings: Bold (700)
  • Body text: Regular (400)
