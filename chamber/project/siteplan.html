Lagos Food Finder - Site Plan

Site Name

Lagos Food Finder

This name was chosen because the website helps users easily find popular foods and restaurants in Lagos, Nigeria.

Site Purpose

The Lagos Food Finder website provides information about local Nigerian dishes and restaurants in Lagos. It helps users discover where to eat, what to eat, and how much meals cost. The site also includes weather updates to help users plan their outings.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find affordable local food near me in Lagos?
  • What are the most popular Nigerian dishes I should try?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: #2E8B57 (Sea Green) - Used for headers, navigation, and accents.

Secondary Color: #F4A261 (Light Orange) - Used for backgrounds and highlights.

Typography

Font: Roboto

  • Headings: Bold Roboto
  • Body Text: Regular Roboto
